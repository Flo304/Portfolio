What is Syn? Syn focuses on creating opportunities to connect with non-human organisms in urban environments, through the use of scientific knowledge and prototyping. Nature is fascinating and functions in different ways than our human brain or computation. Sometimes things are so complex that they are indistinguishable from *magic*. Let's investigate the micro world to understand it better at our human scale.
